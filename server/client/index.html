Bio

Anna Goldenberg is a multimedia journalist publishing in both English and German. Anna is currently completing her graduate degree in Digital Media at the Columbia University Graduate School of Journalism, which gave her the chance to pursue the widest variety of stories possible using an Olympus recorder, a DSLR camera and a notepad. She reported from polling sites, civil courts, yeshivas, Scrabble tournaments and neuroscience laboratories for Brooklyncampaign.com, The New York World and Uptown Radio.

Born and bred in Austria, Anna earned her B.A. in Politics, Psychology and Sociology at the University of Cambridge, UK, where she wrote for the student newspapers Varsity and The Cambridge Student. Anna completed internships at the Austrian news publications Die Presse and profil and is a regular contributor for profil wissen, the Austrian science magazine.

Anna Goldenberg schreibt, fotografiert und mixt Geschichten in Englisch und in Deutsch. Ihre journalistischen Arbeiten erschienen bisher u.a. in den österreichischen Nachrichtenpublikationen Presse und profil. Anna ist freie Mitarbeite Für das österreichische Wissenschaftsmagazin
